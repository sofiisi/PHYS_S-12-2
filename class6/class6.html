For the photogrammetry app, I used Qlone, which requires printing a mat. To get a better measurement, I printed both the horizontal and the vertical ones. The app works with q dome style in which they show you what parts are already recognized. First, I tried with the small bottle because it had a lot of details that would be tedious to create in the fusion 360. Sadly, the app couldn't get an accurate model, even though I use different measurements in various positions. For those reasons, I change to a simpler body; in this case, the app got it, okay, but with the edition tool, I could manually perfect the figure. Still, I couldn't get the exact shape. I wouldn't recommend this app because it took so much time to take the measurements for the results that I gave you. Here are some pictures of the process
