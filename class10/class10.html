Vending machine

For my machine, I decided to make a vending machine that works with a website. The materials I used were: two stepper motors, two motor drivers, a breadboard, jumper wires, the huzzah board, a ton of cardboard, wire, hot glue, and tape. I used this video as inspiration, but I change the measurements and the circuit.

First, I build the circuit and code it with a simple program to ensure that it works. Then I made the base structure and molded the wires. Here I committed a mistake because the end of the wire wasn't center, so when the motor turned, the movement was displaced. When I had everything glue at the inside, I added the other half of the box and the front face. I would love to put plastic in the upper window, but I didn't have any.





On the other hand, I worked on the code. We can divide this into two parts the Arduino and the JavaScript inside the HTML. With the first one, I connected the board to the firebase and used the stepper library to make the motors turn when the database was ”on”.



In the second one, I used the example from class 9 and edited it to change the variable names. Then I realized that to make the motors stop when they did one cycle, I needed to edit the JavaScript, not the Arduino. To make this possible, I added a setTimeout between the two updates to the database. I also printed in the console when it turns on and off to have control and know if it was working how I wanted.
